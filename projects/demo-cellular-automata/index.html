Cellular worlds - demonstration page

Cellular worlds is a collection of cellullar automata displaying complex and varied animated patterns.

These animations are especially well suited to LED walls with a relatively large pitch, where each LED can play the role of a grid cell.

The automaton can be interacted with by moving the mouse over the canvas while holding the Shift key down. This will activate any cell moused over. Clicking while holding down various combinations of the Shift and Ctrl keys also affects the automaton. In applications, this feature could be coupled to motion sensors.

Cellular worlds is customisable in various ways.

  • The colors cycle smoothly by default, but they can be changed at will.
  • The patterns come in various styles, uniquely identified by the style identifier below.
  • A logo can be incorporated in the animation. Given a logo provided as a black and white image, different styles can be applied on the black and white portion of the image, integrating the logo organically into the pattern.
  • Other automata, with different dynamics, are available upon request.

Cellular worlds - demo

Cellular worlds is a collection of cellullar automata displaying complex and varied animated patterns.

It is customisable in various ways. The colors cycle smoothly by default, but can changed at will. A logo as a black and white image can be overlaid on the grid and different coloring rules be used on the black and white portion of the image, integrating the logo organically into the pattern.

The automaton can be interacted with by moving the mouse over the canvas while holding the Shift key down. This will activate any cell you mouse over. Clicking while holding down various combinations of the Shift and Ctrl keys also affects the automaton.

This animation is especially well suited to LED walls with a relatively large pitch, where each LED can play the role of a grid cell.
